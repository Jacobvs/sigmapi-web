Rush 2017

Click calendar to expand image

Get excited for Rush 2017! All unaffiliated WPI students are welcome to join us at our chapter house (34 Institute Road / 17 Dean Street) for lunch, dinner, and events. Expect great food and fun while meeting the Brothers and other rushees!

Also, check out our Facebook Rush page for news on meals, events, and everything else Rush-related.

  • Philanthropy: Amazing Day

    Sigma Pi's Amazing Day event on the WPI quad raised awareness for suicide prevention and accepted donations in support of Amazing Day, Sigma Pi International's philanthropic organization.

  • Graduation 2017

    Congratulations to Sigma Pi's twenty-eight seniors and one graduate student, who graduated in May 2017!

  • Orchid Ball

    Held on a yacht in Boston Harbor, this year's Orchid Ball featured delicious food, fun dancing, and beautiful sunset views of the Boston skyline.

  • Relay for Life

    We participated in the ACS's Relay for Life at WPI in order to raise funds and support for cancer awareness, research, and treatment.
